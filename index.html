Week 1–2 Summary & Action Plan
📋

SUMMARY — What We Learned

5 key findings
✅
Lip Serum & Skin Tint are the only profitable SKUs. These two products are the only ones consistently above 1.0× ROI. Lip Serum drives 31% of all orders at the lowest CPO ($9.41) — it is the engine of the whole account right now.
✅
Retinol education content has breakthrough potential. @59andflawless hit ROI 5.21× with a long-form "retinol isn't scary" video — the highest-efficiency creative in the entire dataset. CVR 8.8% and 2s view rate 56.8% confirm that educational content holds attention and converts.
✅
Jun 17 proves the model can scale. 73 orders at $10 CPO and 1.36× ROI on the last day shows the algorithm is learning — higher budget on proven creatives leads to better efficiency, not worse.
⚠️
Only 27% of creatives (55 out of 206) generated any sales. The creative pool is large but conversion is highly concentrated in the top 10 videos. We are paying for exposure on 73% of videos that don't convert.
❌
Peeling Gel is a budget drain. ROI 0.37×, CPO $32.92 — nearly 3.5× more expensive per order than Lip Serum. 12 consecutive days of underperformance with no recovery signal.
✅

TO-DO — Action Items for This Week

5 actions
1
Pause Peeling Gel campaign immediately URGENT
Stop spending on Peeling Gel (ROI 0.37×, CPO $32.92). Reallocate that budget directly into Lip Serum and Skin Tint campaigns. Do not resume until new, stronger creatives are ready and tested.
2
Increase budget on Lip Serum & Skin Tint HIGH
These are the only 2 SKUs above 1.0× ROI. Push daily budget up by at least 30–50% on both campaigns. Monitor CPO — if it stays under $12, keep scaling.
3
Brief new creators on Retinol education angle HIGH
Recruit 3–5 creators to replicate the @59andflawless format: long-form, educational, "retinol doesn't have to be scary" narrative. This angle has proven 5.21× ROI and 8.8% CVR — the highest in the dataset. Focus on creators with skincare-educated audiences.
4
Replace silent clay demos with emotional outcome hooks MEDIUM
Brief clay stick creators away from silent product demos. Instead, lead with the emotional result: "I never knew a clay stick would make my skin this clear 😭" style (see @wellnessbenefitsonly, ROI 1.84×). Provide specific hook script templates when briefing.
5
Stop spreading budget across low-converting creatives MEDIUM
73% of creatives (151 videos) generated zero sales. Review which videos are still receiving budget with no conversions and pause them. Concentrate ad spend on the top 15 converting creatives. Quality over quantity.
